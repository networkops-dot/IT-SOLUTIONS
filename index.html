Who We Are

The Islands' IT Backbone Since 2008

IT Solutions is a telecom and IT infrastructure service provider headquartered in Port Blair, Andaman & Nicobar Islands. Since 2008, we have been the region's most trusted partner for wireless communication systems, enterprise networking, CCTV surveillance, fire alarm systems, and fully integrated IT solutions.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we have delivered mission-critical projects for government institutions, hospitality groups, corporate organizations, and commercial establishments spanning the archipelago — from Port Blair to the remote islands of the Nicobar district.
